Rewind

Strings are an important component of coding. Knowing how to reverse them may seem trivial when compared to real world applications but this knowledge does have ancillary, if not always functional, worth.

There are some who believe string reversal is purely an academic pursuit. While it’s practical use in the wild may be rare it is not altogether unheard of. Nor is it useless knowledge. Knowing how to reverse a string does demonstrate a basic understanding of strings, arrays, for loops, etc. It is also a common subject during interviews, making it’s worth far more tangible for the novice.

The “decrementing for loop” is not the only method available to reverse a string. The “split, Join, Reverse”, “Recursion”, “Array, Prototype, reduce” and “Regular Expressions” are all viable options as well.
